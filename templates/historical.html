Earthquake Explorer Challenge:

Question: Imagine you are a geologist exploring earthquakes. Choose a country on the map, set the minimum and maximum years, and select a specific level of earthquake strength. What patterns or insights did you discover about earthquakes in that region? Share your findings with the class. Use the capture button to share an image of your exploration.

Map Mastery:

Question: Take a closer look at our dynamic map. Zoom in on a region and observe the changes in earthquake information. How does the zoom level affect the details you see on the map? Share your observations with your classmates and discuss why this feature helps understand earthquake data.

Top Ten Challenge:

Question: Explore the section on the top ten countries with the highest historical earthquake magnitudes. Choose one of the countries listed and research further. What interesting facts can you find about earthquakes in that country? Prepare a short presentation to share with the class, highlighting the most fascinating information you discovered.
